Account Deletion Request

If you wish to delete your EZSpeech account, please follow the steps below:

  • 1. Send an email to our support team at mila4ai@gmail.com.
  • 2. In your email, include your full name, registered email address, and request for account deletion.

Once we receive your request, we will process the account deletion and notify you by email.

Data Deletion and Retention:

- Upon deletion, all personal data associated with your account will be permanently removed from our database.

- Usage and transactional records may be retained for a period of up to 90 days for compliance and record-keeping purposes.
